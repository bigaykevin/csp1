Advanced Marine Calculator for Sailors

NavEssentials compiles a wide range of navigational computation in one place for you to use
Dead Reckoning | Mercator

Determine current position by projecting course and speed from previous observed position

ETA | Required Speed

Ascertain estimated time of arrival and determine which speed to sail to reach ETA

Gyro Error

Calculate Gyro Compass Error by Sun's azimuth and check sunrise/sunset by entering time and date

Great Circle Sailing

Plot the shortest route along a great circle track and modify to limit maximum latitude

Passage Planning

Develop a complete description of the voyage from port of departure to destination

Anchor Holding Power

Ensure safe anchoring by establishing vessel's anchor holding power and critical wind velocity

Tide Tables | Streams

Tidal height, timing and stream data to aid in planning Arrival and Departure times in port

Marine Unit Conversion

Access a compiled selection of commonly used conversion in the maritime world

Recognized by trusted maritime organizations
